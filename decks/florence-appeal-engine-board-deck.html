Risks and Mitigations
We have anticipated and addressed every material risk before launch
Fee-Splitting / Anti-Kickback
Revenue-share arrangements in healthcare billing and collections are standard practice and well-established in the prior authorization and RCM industry. Not novel legal territory.
✓ Mitigation: EBG retained for legal review. Rev-share is industry standard in PA industry.
InterQual / MCG Licensing
We reference the logic and clinical criteria frameworks without reproducing proprietary content. Hemant's independent clinical expertise provides the validation layer that doesn't require licensing.
✓ Mitigation: Reference criteria logic only. Hemant's expertise is independent and unencumbered.
Payer Pushback at Scale
If payers reduce frivolous denials because appeals become automated, that is a feature — not a bug. Aligned with CMS policy goals. Overwhelm of the denial system forces payers to improve criteria.
✓ Mitigation: Payer behavior change = reduced denials = better patient outcomes. Win-win.
Care Management Distraction
Existing care management customers continue uninterrupted. A separate engineering sprint is allocated to the appeal engine. No shared resources between the two workstreams during the 90-day pilot.
✓ Mitigation: Separate engineering sprint. Existing customers fully supported throughout.
